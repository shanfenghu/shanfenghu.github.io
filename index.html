shanfeng2.hu [at] northumbria.ac.uk, Google Scholar, LinkedIn

Lecturer (The Why Lab), Jun 2020 - Present
PhD, Oct 2016 - Aug 2019 (awarded in Mar 2020)
Computer and Information Sciences Department
Northumbria University

Research Themes: I’m running the Why Lab to work on Causal Machine Learning and its applications to real-world processes (e.g., supply chains, smart manufacturing) for value creation. Particularly, I’m interested in understanding and tracking the socio-economic impacts of low-carbon policies through the lens of causal inference. Fairness of such policies is at the core of my study.

Northumbria Kagglers Club

I founded the Kaggle club at Northumbria University in Feb 2021. We now have 40 student members (including UGs, MSc, PhDs) and 5 staff members in the club. We have hosted the following rounds of competitions within our students:

  • Round 1 - Automatic Diabetes Diagnosis
    • 3 teams all won the Gold medal, 8 teams of 12 students submitted
  • Round 2 - Who Survived on Titanic
    • 1 team won the Gold medal, 2 teams won the Silver, 9 teams of 14 students submitted
  • Round 3a - Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals Certificate Exam
    • 4 students have successfully obtained the certificates as of 14th May 2021 and won the Gold medal
  • Round 3b - Pneumonia Screening from Chest X-Ray - ongoing
  • Round 4a - UK Azure Fundamentals Championship 2021 - ongoing

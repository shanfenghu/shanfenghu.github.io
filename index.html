Lecturer of Machine Learning, Jun 2020 - Present
Computer and Information Sciences Department
Northumbria University

I graduated as a PhD from Northumbria University in Mar 2020. During my study time I also worked as a Senior Data Scientist for Tenokonda between Dec 2018 and Apr 2020. My research is centered around the key challenge of bringing AI from the current narrow (task-dependent) generalization to a truely broad generalization across tasks. I am also exploring the use of computational modelling and machine learning to understand human cognition and socio-economic behaviors in the society. Deploying AI to real-world processes attracts me too.
